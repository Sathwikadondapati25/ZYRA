How Zyra works

From a few inputs to a living, data-informed brand system.

1

Describe your brand

Enter your industry, audience, values, and style. Zyra uses this as the foundation for your brand identity.

2

Generate identity & assets

3

Technical architecture

Designed for scalability, security, and real-time AI-assisted branding.

Backend

FastAPI (Python) orchestrates AI workflows and exposes APIs for branding, trend analysis, and sentiment insights.

Frontend

React-based web client (HTML, CSS, JS) provides an intuitive interface for configuring and refining brand identities.

AI models

Hugging Face / Azure OpenAI for text and sentiment; Stable Diffusion / RunwayML for logo and visual concept generation.

Trend & competitor analysis

LangChain + Pinecone for storing and querying embeddings of social and competitor data in real time.

Brand dashboards

D3.js visualizations for sentiment over time, content performance, and competitive positioning.

Cloud deployment

Runs on IBM Cloud or Azure with support for secure, multi-tenant access and future enterprise integrations.
